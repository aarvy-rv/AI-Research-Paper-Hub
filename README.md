# AI Research Paper Hub

AI Research Paper Hub helps learners explore important AI, machine learning, deep learning, and generative AI research papers without feeling lost.

The app organizes papers into a guided learning path so visitors can understand where to start, what to read next, and how different areas of AI connect. Each paper page includes the paper, reading resources, progress tracking, and a ChatGPT discussion handoff that lets users study the paper with their own ChatGPT account.

Visit the app:

https://airesearchpaperhub.online

## What You Can Do

- Follow a beginner-friendly AI/ML paper roadmap.
- Browse papers by learning path, topic, concept, or year.
- Open and read full research papers when available.
- Prefer arXiv paper and PDF links, with legitimate alternatives when arXiv is unavailable.
- Track papers as unread, reading, or read.
- Save your personal reading progress after signing in.
- Use Google sign-in or email/password authentication.
- Open a structured ChatGPT prompt for any paper to discuss and understand it more deeply.
- Explore extra learning resources such as videos, Google Scholar, and Papers with Code.

## Who It Is For

This app is for students, self-learners, engineers, researchers, and anyone trying to build a structured understanding of AI research papers.

Instead of showing a random list of papers, the app acts like a study companion: start with landmark work, move through foundations, then branch into LLMs, GenAI, agents, systems, safety, and interpretability.
